William Reed Business Media

I produced sets of interactive web banners for many different events using both GIF and SWF files.

During my employment at Suite Creative, I have with William Reed Business Media to deliver web banner campaigns for several of their events.

The banners ranged from static JPG files to fully animated SWF files created using Adobe Flash. The animated banners featured clean fade transitions and were fully clickable in order to take the user to the event.

During my work with WRBM, I produced banners of all different sizes and some were even full site takeovers. I also ensured that each banner I delivered met the client's and website specifications which included file size and pixel dimensions.

Some of the clients included:

  • The Restaurant Show

  • The Forecourt Show

  • The National Convenience Show

  • Farm Shop & Deli Show
